The Full Story

Medicines are the necessity of humans in today’s world. It is required for every age group, either a small child, an adult, or a senior citizen. As we have seen Covid – 19 lowered the immunity of people and was very fatal too. Medicines are a key to saving us and our family from diseases and living a long life. There are many situations where people see their loved ones suffer because there is no medicine and even lose them just because there was a shortage of them. So, to lessen the pain of seeing your loved ones suffer or losing them we bring you the most appropriate site/ app where the people would be connected to the nearest medical or chemist who would deliver the asked medicine at your doorstep within minutes if emergency or within 1 – 3 hours.

Purpose

1. Convenience: Instamedi make it easy for patients to order and receive their prescribed medications from the comfort of their homes.
​ 2. Accessibility: Instamedi provide access to a wide range of medications, including over the counter and prescription drugs, as well as medical supplies. Patients can quickly search for the products they need without the need to physically visit a pharmacy.
​ 3. Time-saving: By eliminating the need for in-person visits to a pharmacy, Instamedi save patients’ time. This can be crucial, particularly for those with busy schedules or those who require ongoing medications.
